CLASS STRUCTURE

Our school is structured to support students at every stage of their primary education journey. We offer a developmentally approriate progression through the following three(3) key levels.

APPLICATION FORMS are available at the school premises or through online applications by
sending a request through the school e-mail address

PRE-PRIMARY

This foundational stage focuses on early childhood development through play-based and hands on learning. It is divided into baby class, middle class and top-class.

MIDDLE PRIMARY

In these formative years, students build on early learning with a more structured curriculum. Emphasis is placed on developing reading writing, numeracy skills, creativity, critical thinking and personal responsibility.
This is P1 to P4.

UPPER PRIMARY

Between P5 and P7, students begin to engage with more complex concepts across core subjects including mathematics, science , social studies and language arts.
There is a growing focus on independent learning preparing for secondary school.
